Mont Blanc, meaning "White Mountain", is the highest mountain in the Apls and the highest in Europe west of Russia`s Caucasus peaks. It rise 4,808.7 m (15,777 ft) above sea level and is ranked 11th in the world in topographic prominence. The mountain stands in a range called the Graian Alps, between the regions og Aostra Valley, and Savoine and Houte-Savoie, France.
